Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Guitar Explorer ("the app") is a personal music theory and chord library tool. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Google profile ID

We also store data you create in the app:

  • Songs and chord sheets you add to your library
  • Sets and playlists you create
  • Practice progress you log
  • App preferences (theme, view mode)

2. How We Use It

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality — syncing your library across devices. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your library data is stored in Supabase (supabase.com), a secure cloud database. Your session is stored in your browser's local storage. Uploaded files and chord sheets are stored in your browser locally and in Supabase if you are signed in.

4. AI Analysis

When you upload a chord sheet for AI analysis, the image or PDF is sent to Anthropic's Claude API via a Cloudflare Worker. The content is processed to extract chord data and is not stored by Anthropic beyond the immediate API call.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in only
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic Claude API — chord sheet analysis
  • Cloudflare Workers — API proxy

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by signing in and using the Clear Library option, or by contacting us directly. You can sign out at any time to stop session data being stored.

7. Children

Guitar Explorer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
